How We're Pitching This

OpenAI is building into the talent and workforce space from multiple directions simultaneously — a Jobs Platform, AI certifications, enterprise HR agents, workforce planning tools, and an AI super-assistant that takes actions on behalf of users. Every one of those initiatives needs the same thing underneath: clean, structured, production-grade talent data.

That's what Censia is. We're not pitching a competing product — we're pitching infrastructure. The intelligence layer that makes OpenAI's talent initiatives real on day one instead of requiring years of data collection, taxonomy building, and model training to get to production quality.

The core message: OpenAI has the best models in the world. They don't have 550M enriched talent profiles, a self-correcting skills taxonomy, or 8 years of career trajectory data. Building that from scratch takes longer than their mid-2026 Jobs Platform timeline allows. The math only works with a partner.
