## How We're Pitching This

OpenAI is building into the talent and workforce space from multiple directions simultaneously. A Jobs Platform, AI certifications, enterprise HR agents, workforce planning tools, and an AI super-assistant that takes actions on behalf of users. Every one of those initiatives needs the same thing underneath: structured, production-grade talent intelligence.

That's what Censia is. We're not pitching a competing product. We're pitching **the domain intelligence layer**. The specialized models and methods that structure talent data so OpenAI's general-purpose models produce results worth trusting.

The core message: **OpenAI has the best models in the world. But the talent vertical is deceptively complex. The same title means different things across companies. Skills are described hundreds of ways. What a role required 8 years ago is fundamentally different from today. That complexity defeats general-purpose approaches. Censia has spent 8 years building the domain-specific models that resolve it. Everyone has access to profiles. The difference is understanding what they mean. Building that depth of vertical expertise from scratch takes longer than their timeline allows. The math only works with a partner.**
