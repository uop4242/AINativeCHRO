Speaker Notes

Behind the data we just showed you, there's a purpose-built AI and machine learning engine. Let me walk you through the four layers. First, the embedding foundation — we train custom transformer architectures using contrastive learning to create domain-specific vector representations for every entity type: skills, titles, companies, industries. These aren't off-the-shelf models — they understand the language of talent. Second, curation and labeling — we've built custom data pipelines with expert-in-the-loop validation. Every model output passes through quality gates with visualization tools that maintain precision and explainability at scale. Third, knowledge models — this is where structure emerges. Automated taxonomy induction discovers skill hierarchies, graph learning maps relationships between entities, and semantic extraction builds the knowledge graph that connects everything. And fourth, ranking and scoring — learning-to-rank models, similarity scoring, and multi-signal fusion that optimize match quality across our entire talent dataset. These four layers work together as a unified system — each one feeding the next. Every capability we show you in the following slides is powered by this stack.
