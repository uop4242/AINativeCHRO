Workforce Intelligence Infrastructure, Built for Enterprise Reality

Verizon, a global telecom provider, had a bold vision: enable personalized career mobility for every one of its 75,000 employees. Censia delivered the foundation by turning fragmented workforce signals into Golden Records, normalized and enriched at scale with skills, proficiency scores, trajectory context, and other decision-ready signals. This made it possible for Verizon to drive measurable results across internal mobility, avoidable attrition, and career path visibility.

The Problem

Only 5% of employees had skills listed in their profiles, which meant:

  • Employees lacked clear career paths and next-step guidance.
  • Skills weren’t translated into adjacent roles or growth steps.
  • Upskilling lacked clear targets tied to real role requirements.
  • The organization couldn’t quantify gaps or identify succession-ready candidates.
From Data to Action

Censia delivered AI-enriched Golden Records across the enterprise, turning role, experience, and market signals into inferred skills, proficiency, and career intelligence. Profiles were completed and standardized at scale without manual entry, giving every team a fair, decision-ready foundation for mobility and development.
