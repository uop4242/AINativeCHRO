Speaker Notes

Before you can analyze gaps or map pathways, you need a common language. And skills are the messiest data in HR. One concept can appear as hundreds of variants. The same acronym means different things in different contexts. Skills emerge and sunset over time. And every company describes them differently. This is the problem we solve.
