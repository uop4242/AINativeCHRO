Role Overview

The Software Engineer II position at Forward Advantage Credit Union is a delivery-oriented engineering role built around converting prioritized business requirements into production-grade software that is secure, stable, and regulatory-compliant. This role supports both member-facing and staff-facing capabilities across business lines, with a standing expectation that all engineering work follows robust risk management, disciplined SDLC processes, and traceable delivery documentation.

Operating within an Agile squad, the Software Engineer II assumes ownership of user stories from backlog grooming through production release. This encompasses defining acceptance criteria, converting requirements into clean and maintainable code, confirming behavior via automated tests, and merging changes seamlessly across systems and architectural layers. The role must operate within enterprise delivery guardrails-change controls, security mandates, documentation norms, and validation checkpoints-while still supporting frequent, dependable releases.

Given that Forward Advantage Credit Union operates at significant scale within a tightly regulated sector, engineering success goes beyond feature delivery alone. The Software Engineer II is expected to build with operational durability in mind: well-defined interfaces, predictable deployment patterns, consistent build and test indicators, and production observability that enables rapid diagnosis and safe resolution. Cross-functional partnership is essential-this role engages closely with Product Owners to validate that intent is accurately captured, with QA/Test Engineering to broaden automated coverage and minimize defect leakage, with DevOps/Platform counterparts to achieve repeatable CI/CD workflows, and with Risk/Compliance stakeholders to integrate required controls early and preserve traceability.

This position generally carries no people-management duties, though influence expands through technical expertise, dependability, and skill in coordinating across stakeholders-particularly when triaging defects, mitigating risk on complex changes via spikes and proofs of concept, and confirming release readiness. Over time, the Software Engineer II is expected to elevate team benchmarks for code quality, testing rigor, pipeline reliability, and documentation habits that foster reuse and operational supportability.

Within Forward Advantage Credit Union's strategic focus on digital engagement and applied AI at scale, the Software Engineer II contributes by delivering well-tested features on predictable timelines, streamlining member servicing workflows, and boosting staff and platform efficiency-all while upholding the security and control standards expected of a regulated financial institution.
